Workflow & best practices.

Recommended technique

  • Insert horizontally until the teeth seat, then lift slightly or pull level.
  • Guide rolls along rails, a dolly, or a waiting forklift to prevent core drag.
  • Never pull downward—downward angles add friction and can tear the core wall.

Operational benefits

  • One operator unloads full containers in minutes.
  • Reduces shoulder/back injuries from prying rolls manually.
  • Protects expensive rolls from being rewound due to crushed cores.

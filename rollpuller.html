Safe, repeatable unloading workflow.

Recommended technique

  • Insert the Roll Puller™ horizontally into the core until the teeth seat.
  • Lift slightly or pull level with the container floor—avoid downward angles that add drag.
  • Guide the roll along rails, a dolly, or a waiting forklift to keep cores intact.

Benefits

  • One operator can unload an entire container without manual wrestling.
  • Less risk of shoulder or back injuries from prying rolls loose.
  • Reduces core damage, saving money on rewinds or replacements.

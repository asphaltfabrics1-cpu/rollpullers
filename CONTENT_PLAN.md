## RollPullers Content Framework

Use this document to copy/paste section prompts into Codex/Claude when generating the actual site. It mirrors the inline comments but keeps everything in one place for quick reference.

### index.html
- Hero: "World's best telescoping paving-fabric installation machines" + decades of innovation, 60+ countries.
- Innovation highlights: 6 patents, since early 1980s, focus on laydown unit efficiency.
- Featured product cards: Grizzly Series, Roll Puller™, Accessories, Custom Equipment, Consulting.
- Benefits grid: wrinkle-free laydown, faster installs, obstruction-friendly, safer crews, lower labor.
- Evolution ribbon: mention ongoing upgrades (2019–2024 kits, multi-bar tension, roller systems).
- CTA/footer: invite quote requests; placeholders for [Contact Email] and [Phone].

### grizzly.html
- Overview comparing Grizzly 600T vs Grizzly Cub 300T.
- Specs table: telescoping ranges (up to ~20 ft vs ~15.5 ft), short-roll coverage, transport widths, mount compatibility.
- Feature sections:
  - Double-bar frame & universal mounts.
  - Telescoping dual arms on bearings (optional hydraulic kit).
  - Brush system (articulating center mount + jack leveling + extensions + polypropylene brushes).
  - Rotating spindle braking roll holders + affordable wear parts.
  - Solid steel cones for ~3–5 in cores.
  - Crab-claw swing arms (dual/optional hydraulic) for curb/guardrail installs.
  - Dual-bar telescoping PVC tensioning + optional dual-PVC roller for grids.
  - Third middle arm for short rolls (1–10 ft / 1–8 ft) centered/off-centered.
  - Oil-truck mounting option.
- Upgrades 2019–2024: articulating center brush mount (4 positions), multi-bar tension plate, improved telescoping bearings, spindle upgrades.
- CTA linking to contact page.

### rollpuller.html
- Intro explaining unloading purpose.
- Model comparison (RP3XL vs RP4XL core diameters).
- Key advantages: 2016 redesign, deeper core grip, laser-cut armor teeth, heavy-duty shaft/forged ring.
- Proper usage guidance (horizontal/slight lift only; no downward pulls).
- Safety/productivity benefits (single-operator unloading, reduced injury risk).
- Optional demo/spec sheet link placeholder.
- CTA to contact for pricing/distribution.

### accessories.html
- Geotextile brush story: 16" & 24" hardwood blocks, 30% more filaments, higher melt point (~325°F), no wrinkle pinching, optional chevron/straight configurations.
- Dual-PVC roller/tension upgrade explanation with slider + radius adjustments (pins 0–45°) for grids/composites.
- Replacement parts catalog: brake pads, T-screws, neoprene bearings, cone sharpening, telescoping hardware, swing-arm hydraulics.
- Retrofit note for legacy bolt-together units.
- CTA linking to contact details/placeholders.

### custom-equipment.html
- Overview of bespoke fabrication for landfills, stabilization, slopes, hover-head/cable systems, multi-roll rigs.
- Process: discovery → design → fabrication → commissioning.
- Sample solutions + results (tight clearances, multi-roll installs, oil truck integration beyond base option).
- CTA for custom consultation ([Contact Email]/[Phone]).

### consulting.html
- Services summary: on-site consulting, HQ/virtual training, design/spec assistance, conference education.
- Logistics: flexible session lengths, global availability, bundles with upgrades/custom builds.
- Value: reduced rework, faster crew ramp, compliance with DOT specs.
- CTA for proposals (placeholders).

### contact.html
- Friendly invitation to reach out (contractors, DOTs, distributors).
- Placeholder address/email/phone.
- Optional contact form (name, company, email, phone, project details) with dummy action.
- Secondary CTA for spec sheets or consulting schedule.

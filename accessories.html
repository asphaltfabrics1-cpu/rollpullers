Brushes, tension systems, and service kits.

Keep Grizzly installations running smooth with patent-pending brushes, dual-PVC roller systems, and replacement hardware that drops into both modern and legacy machines.

Geotextile brush lineup

16" & 24" hardwood blocks

Each block ships with a threaded insert and bolt for quick swaps in the field. Brushes include ~30% more polypropylene filament for full coverage and higher durability on hot days (rated to ~325°F).

Chevron or straight configurations

Choose angled chevron brushes to shed debris and keep seams tight, or straight brushes for traditional sweeping patterns. Extensions cover laydowns beyond 18 ft.

Brushes are compatible with both Grizzly 600T and Grizzly Cub 300T assemblies.

Dual-PVC roller tension system

A patent-pending upgrade that adds slider-and-radius adjustments to dial in fabric stretch before brushing. Pin the tension bars between 0° and 45° for different materials, then let the roller module pre-stretch grids, composites, or hybrid mats.

Slider controls

Move the bar along laser-cut slots to match roll width and stiffness.

Radius plate

Adjust the wrap angle so fabrics with high elongation seat properly.

Retrofit friendly

Installs on telescoping Grizzlies and bolt-together legacy units with minimal fabrication.

Replacement & service kits

Rotating spindle rebuild

Brake pads, T-screws, nuts, and neoprene bearings to keep roll holders smooth.

Telescoping hardware

New bearings, seals, and tube sections for mainframe arms or hydraulic conversions.

Swing-arm & hydraulic kits

Mechanical or hydraulic crab-claw arms plus hardware for both sides of the machine.

Solid cone service

Replacement cones for 3–5 in cores or sharpening services for existing sets.
